Cold Water

Description

I was speaking to Lady Lywis-Smythe after the incident. We were situated in her dining room, the late May sunlight streaming through the window, catching the motes of dust dancing in the sunbeam. Dear Aggy was speaking in hushed, urgent tones about barristers, her eyes kept glancing to the the door, presumably watching for the lingering eyes of the help.

I was not ungrateful for her wise words of wisdom, nor for her gracious hosting given the circumstances, but my eyes were transfixed on the objet d'art I beheld.

"Good Lord Aggy, what in heavens name is that!?" I could restrain myself no longer.

"My dear Charles, stop changing the subject, please listen to me!"

When she saw how captivated I had become, she finally revealed the secret I hand hungered for.

"What has gotten into you? That is just a glass of water!"

Water.

Agua vita.

The stuff of life.

My dear readers, you can be rest assured I put a halt to all other topics of conversation until that damned woman (and my dearest frien)d relinquished the recipe into my possession.

Here I bequeath it to you.

  • Water
  • Optional: Ice
